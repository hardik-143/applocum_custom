Any Questions For Us

We are transparent in all walks of business from billing through to the quality of the locums we provide. We wave the flag for the NHS and ensure we do everything to provide first class high quality solutions to the staffing crisis.

Account (1)
How do I change my password?

You can change your password via the online portal, this cannot be done via the app. Simply log out and click “forgot password” in order to be redirected to the site.

Candidate (4)
How do I redeem my apployalty points?

The app will alert you when you have hit your milestone.

How do I check my apployalty points?

If you log into your AppLocum app, you can track your AppLoyalty points from the shifts you have worked.

How do I change my payment details?

Just login to the secure AppLocum app, go to settings, then bank details to update your details where appropriate. We encourage all locums to keep their bank details up to date to ensure you get paid correctly and as quickly as possible.

When will I be paid?

We pay the working week after your shift!

Client (1)
How do I create job preferences?

Within the settings section, you will find the “Job preferences” tab – within this you are able to select available dates, travel distance preference and surgery type. Once completed this will be saved and applied to your job searches so only the most relevant ones will show on your job search.

Client & Candidate (1)
Are there any charges?

No, you will not be charged for joining AppLocum or using the AppLocum app!

Compliance & Candidate (1)
How can I upload my documents?

All of your documents can be uploaded through the profile section of the AppLocum app.

Compliance questions (1)
How can I upload my documents?

All of your documents can be uploaded through the profile section of the AppLocum app.

Compliance questions a (1)
What compliance documents do I need to start looking for work?
Compliance Document Other Details
CV with/and 2 clinical references

Including clinical references, you must have worked with within the last 3 years.

Enhanced DBS (Within the last 12 months recommended, no more than 3 years)

You can apply for a DBS through us if needed, and get signed onto the online renewal service. For more information, please contact compliance@applocum.com or 0161 711 0655.

Indemnity insurance (MDU, MPS, MDUUS, …)

You should have a letter/email detailing exactly what you are covered for and for how long.

Right to work in the UK

British/EU passport (residency permit if applicable). UK driving licence and birth certificate.

GMC CCT/MRCGP/JCPTGP/PMETB
Immunisations (HepB and TB)

If required, we can send a TB form for another healthcare professional to complete

BLS / ALS / ILS

Within the last 12 months, unless expiry stated on certificate

Contract (3)
I worked different hours than arranged

You can amend your time sheet to make it correct at any time before it gets signed off by the client. The client will be notified of the amended times and sign this off.

How do I find my booked jobs?

All of your booked jobs are saved within the app in the “my jobs” section. You will see them saved within the “booked” tab.

How to find out more about your session?

All the information needed is within the job spec found in the available jobs section. However, if you find you require further information you can contact our friendly advisors at any point.

General (3)
How to add a job arranged through client outside of the app?

The client will send a job request through to you directly. Simply accept the job request for the timesheets and relevant details to be generated.

What is IR35?

IR35 is legislation released by the HMRC to essentially stop tax avoidance via company or partnership. If you fall within IR35, your earnings are subject to PAYE and the appropriate deductions for income tax and National Insurance will be taken.

Can I message through the app?

Within the app there is the ability to instant message all of our departments from the “message” section located on your dashboard. Here you can contact; Accounts, Bookings, Recruitment, Compliance and Administration.

General Questions (3)
How do I change my payment details?

We encourage all locums to keep their bank details up to date to ensure you get paid correctly and as quickly as possible.

All payment details can be found quite easily on your personal portal via the app or website.

How quickly do locums get paid?

AppLocum pays their locums the following working week.

AppLocum charges

AppLocum apply no charges to any service or fee’s for downloading the app. We are completely free.

General, Candidate (4)
I’m unable to work what do I do?

Go to the booked jobs section – cancel your shift. Ensure you inform the recruiter of your absence via the message function.

How do I withdraw from a job?

Simply go on to the job you have requested and booked and click cancel, this will then go through to the surgery.

Can I negotiate the rate?

Go to your applied jobs section, and simply click negotiate rate. Once completed this will go to the clinic for consideration.

How do I find jobs?

Go to the available jobs section. All relevant listings will be ready for you to apply for.

General, Invoice & Contract (1)
There is a problem with my timesheet, what do I do?

To lodge any issues you may have with timesheets, go to the “my jobs” then head to “booked” – select the job you have had an issue with and simply request a call back. You will be taken to a message page where you can express the issue you are facing and this will go to the accounts team who will deal with the issue appropriately.
